Overview

Purpose

I enjoy photography. I like taking pictures of scenery and all of earth's beauty. I have taken pictures of everywhere I've traveled, like the rolling hills of West Virginia and the sandy beaches of Florida. Do you find enjoyment out of admiring the great outdoors as much as I do? Fortunately, I love sharing the pictures that I take with others. I take the time to make sure that all the pictures I share are pleasant to look at. My website will have many spectacular images that will inspire your inner desire to explore.

Audience

My intended audience is a male in his 20s-30s who enjoys the beauty of the world, as well as photography. This man also enjoys exploring the world. He wants to satisfy his desire to view pictures of a variety of landscapes and scenery. He has a deire to learn more about capturing earth's beauty. They want to find out who on the photographers are on the internet, and are interested in learning more about photography from them.

About Me

About Me
Who am I?
My name is Ryan Walker. I was born and raised in West Virginia. I am from a town called Parkersburg, a peaceful place seated alongside the lengthy Ohio River. I am from a family of five. I have two older sisters and both are married. I have fantastic parents, both of which work with special needs students. I am currently attending Brigham Young University as a freshman. I am studying Computer Information Technology, as I greatly enjoy working and tinkering with computers. However, I have a great love photography, as well.
Why I love photography
Taking pictures is one of my favorite hobbies. Being able to capture a moment in time, and then sharing that moment with others is an amazing experience. It allows people to learn and see things from a different perspective. I cherish taking a good picture and admiring it with others. I also love it when others are able to do the same.
Reach out!
If you yourself would like to improve your photography skills, send me a text or email. Even if you just have simple questions about me or photography in general, I am happy to talk with you. Don't be afraid to reach out. During the week, I should be able to get back to you within 24 hours, but please be patient. It is possible that I able out traveling and taking more glorious pictures, so don't be deterred if I am unable to respond right away. Here is my contact information:

Email: wal19059@byui.edu
Thank you for taking the time to visit my website. I can't wait to hear from you!
