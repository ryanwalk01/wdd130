Who am I?

I have two older sisters and both are married. I have fantastic parents, both of which work with special needs students. I am currently attending Brigham Young University as a freshman. I am studying Computer Information Technology, as I greatly enjoy working and tinkering with computers. However, I have a great love photography, as well.

Why I love photography

Taking pictures is one of my favorite hobbies. Being able to capture a moment in time, and then sharing that moment with others is an amazing experience. It allows people to learn and see things from a different perspective. I cherish taking a good picture and admiring it with others. I also love it when others are able to do the same.
