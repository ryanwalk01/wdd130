My Work

I have taken pictures at many locations across the United States. In the state of New York, I have photographed New York City and Niagara Falls. I also have taken plenty of pictures on the beaches of Virginia. I have taken pictures in Florida, the sunshine state, including beaches and grand resorts. I have captured many of the spectacular hills of West Virginia. Finally, I have taken photographs of several places in Utah, where I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. These locations include, Provo, Price, Heber City, and Vernal. I have future plans to travel to even more locations around the world to continue to capture our magnificent earth.
